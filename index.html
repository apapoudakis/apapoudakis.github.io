Argyrios Papoudakis

I am a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh and part of the CDT in NLP program. My supervisors are Prof. Frank Keller and Prof. Mirella Lapata. I am interested in long context (modeling, reasoning and evaluation), text-generation, narratives analysis and multi-modal (language and vision) learning.

Previously, I studied Electrical and Computer Engineering (5-year degree) in Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. I also worked as a research scientist in Visual Computing Lab at CERTH for six months. I was a developer in Google Summer of Code with the Red Hen Lab organization the summer of 2022.
